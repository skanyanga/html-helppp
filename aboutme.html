What I was doing before

July 6th, 5.35pm-the year: 2008, I had just completed the level 2 ICT course. Brimming with confidence and ambition I logically and with no hesitation decided to step up to the level 3 ICT course. Nothing could stop me, or so I thought. Life is full of surprises my dad would always say and I would find that out for the first time, just how much of an affect that could be. Half way into my Level 3 course, I had an opportunity to go and study for an MCSE course for a company with a ready-made job position at the end of the course. After long deliberation I decided to take up the offer and left without finishing my course. A year later the company I was studying with would declare bankruptcy. I was devastated.

Whyy I decided to do this course.

Fast forward July 6th, 5.35pm-2013, four years from the Level 3 ICT course. After years of working in Customer service job roles. I came to realisation that this is not something I want to be doing for the rest of my life nor is it something that I have a passion for. I quickly decided to go back into education and get myself into university in order to get a better future as well as get myself into the IT Sector, something that I have always wanted to be.

Whaat I want to get out of this course.

I’m hoping the course will allow me to advance in my education in to university and cement myself in the IT Sector. I hope this will be more evident in terms of the hard-work I have put in the near future.

I’m hoping g I can reflect back to my younger days and be proud of what I have accomplished and reap the rewards of the hard-work I applied and will continue to apply in the future. Im also hoping to be able to build on the success I have obtained and hopefully start my business either with my partner or on my own. I also believe this course will allow me to learn and study various I.T subjects that I may have not considered such as forensic science which is an interesting subject to go into. In my spare time, I enjoy going out with friends, socialising etc. I am huge cinema buff so the occasional visit to the cinema is a must. I also like to travel around, it makes me feel relaxed and I enjoy seeing new places. “Life is not about being content in one position”.
